# Refunds and Cancellations Policy for Mnyama_Check

**Effective Date:** January 22, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 22, 2024  
**Version:** 1.0.0

## 1. Introduction

This Refunds and Cancellations Policy ("Refund Policy") governs all payments made through the Mnyama_Check mobile application. By making a payment through our App, you agree to this Refund Policy.

All payments are processed through Azampay, our exclusive payment gateway. Please read this policy carefully before making any payments.

## 2. General Refund Principles

### 2.1 Our Commitment
We strive to provide high-quality services and ensure customer satisfaction. If you are not satisfied with a paid service, we will review your case fairly and transparently.

### 2.2 Refund Eligibility
Refunds are considered under the following circumstances:

- **Technical Failure:** If a paid feature fails to work due to our technical error
- **Service Not Delivered:** If you paid for a service that was not provided
- **Duplicate Charges:** If you were charged multiple times for the same service
- **Unauthorized Transaction:** If a payment was made without your authorization

### 2.3 Non-Refundable Items
The following are generally NOT eligible for refunds:

- Services already rendered or utilized
- Subscription periods that have already been accessed
- Promotional or discounted items specifically marked as non-refundable
- One-time purchases after 14 days from the date of purchase
- Change of mind or no longer needing the service
- Failure to use the service due to user error or negligence

## 3. Subscription Cancellations

### 3.1 How to Cancel
You may cancel your subscription at any time through:
- The App settings under "Subscription Management"
- Contacting our support team at support@afyamnyamadigital.co.tz

### 3.2 Cancellation Effects
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
- You will continue to have access to premium features until the end of your paid period
- No partial refunds for mid-cycle cancellations
- Auto-renewal will be disabled immediately upon cancellation

### 3.3 Free Trial Cancellations
If you are on a free trial:
- Cancel at least 24 hours before the trial ends to avoid charges
- No charges will be made if canceled within the trial period
- Once converted to paid, standard cancellation rules apply

## 4. Refund Process

### 4.1 How to Request a Refund
To request a refund, contact our support team at support@afyamnyamadigital.co.tz with:

1. **Your Information:**
   - Registered phone number or email
   - Full name as in the account

2. **Transaction Details:**
   - Transaction ID from Azampay
   - Date and time of transaction
   - Amount paid
   - Service purchased

3. **Reason for Refund:**
   - Detailed explanation of why you're requesting a refund
   - Any supporting evidence (screenshots, error messages)

### 4.2 Refund Review Timeline
- **Initial Response:** Within 24 business hours
- **Investigation Period:** 3-5 business days
- **Final Decision:** Within 7 business days of receiving complete information

### 4.3 Approved Refunds
If your refund is approved:

- Refunds are processed through Azampay to the original payment method
- Processing time: 5-10 business days after approval
- Time to appear in your account: Additional 3-5 business days depending on your bank or mobile money provider
- You will receive a confirmation email with transaction details

### 4.4 Denied Refunds
If your refund request is denied:
- We will provide a detailed explanation
- You may appeal the decision within 7 days
- Further disputes may be escalated through Azampay's dispute resolution

## 5. Special Circumstances

### 5.1 Technical Issues
If you experience technical issues preventing service access:

1. Contact support immediately
2. We will attempt to resolve the issue within 48 hours
3. If unresolved, a refund may be issued for the affected period
4. Refund amount will be prorated based on downtime

### 5.2 Service Discontinuation
If we discontinue a paid service:

- Pro-rated refunds will be issued for remaining periods
- Alternative service offerings may be provided
- 30 days' notice will be given for significant service changes

### 5.3 Price Changes
If we increase subscription prices:

- Existing subscribers get 30 days' notice
- You may cancel before the price increase takes effect
- No refunds for previous periods at old rates

## 6. Azampay Payment Processing

### 6.1 Payment Gateway
All refunds are processed through Azampay. By using our payment services, you agree to:

- Azampay's terms and conditions
- Their refund processing timelines
- Their dispute resolution procedures

### 6.2 Payment Issues
For payment-related issues (failed payments, double charges, etc.):

1. Check with your bank/mobile money provider first
2. Contact Azampay support if the issue persists
3. We will assist in resolving payment gateway issues

### 6.3 Transaction Records
We maintain transaction records for:
- 7 years as required by Tanzanian law
- Customer support purposes
- Financial auditing

## 7. Dispute Resolution

### 7.1 Initial Resolution
Most issues can be resolved by contacting our support team. We commit to:
- Responding within 24 business hours
- Investigating thoroughly
- Providing transparent explanations

### 7.2 Escalation Process
If unsatisfied with our resolution:

1. **Step 1:** Request escalation to a senior support manager
2. **Step 2:** Contact Azampay dispute resolution (for payment issues)
3. **Step 3:** Legal recourse through Tanzanian courts (if applicable)

### 7.3 Good Faith Negotiation
We believe in resolving disputes amicably and will make reasonable efforts to reach a mutually acceptable solution.
