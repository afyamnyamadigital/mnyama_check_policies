# Privacy Policy for Mnyama_Check

**Effective Date:** January 22, 2024  
**Last Updated:** January 22, 2024  
**Version:** 1.0.0

## 1. Introduction

Afya Mnyama Digital Co. Ltd ("we", "our", or "us"), operating through https://afyamnyamadigital.co.tz, is committed to protecting the privacy of users of the Mnyama_Check mobile application ("the app").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the app. By using Mnyama_Check, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
For a better experience while using our application, we may require you to provide certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

- Name
- Phone number or email address
- Unique user ID
- Livestock or farm-related information you submit in the app
- Location data (if you enable location services)

This information is used for:
- User authentication and account management
- Providing veterinary insights and recommendations
- Managing your livestock health records
- Delivering customer support and service updates
- Personalizing your app experience

### 2.2 Log Data
Whenever you use our application, in the event of an error we collect information (through third-party products) called Log Data. This may include:

- Device Internet Protocol (IP) address
- Device name and model
- Operating system version
- App configuration during use
- Date and time of use
- Diagnostic information and other statistics
- Crash reports and performance data

### 2.3 IoT Device Data
If you connect Mnyama_Check to an IoT livestock sensor device, we may collect:

- Temperature readings
- Activity steps and movement patterns
- Health status indicators
- Device battery levels
- Sensor calibration data

These are used only to provide animal health analytics and recommendations.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

- To provide and maintain our Service
- To notify you about changes to our Service
- To allow you to participate in interactive features
- To provide customer support
- To gather analysis or valuable information for improvement
- To monitor the usage of our Service
- To detect, prevent and address technical issues
- To provide you with news, special offers and general information

Mnyama_Check uses Azampay as our exclusive payment gateway. When you make payments through the app:

**Our Data Handling:**

Azampay processes your payment data according to their Privacy Policy, available at: https://azampay.co.tz/privacy-policy

We may employ third-party companies and individuals for:
- Facilitating our Service

- Performing Service-related services

These third parties have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

## 5. Data Security

We value your trust in providing personal and livestock-related information. We use:

- Industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest
- Transport Layer Security (TLS 1.2+) for data in transit
- Firebase security rules and authentication
- Secure communication channels (HTTPS)
- Regular security assessments and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to:

- Provide you with the services you have requested
- Comply with our legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at support@afyamnyamadigital.co.tz. Upon account deletion, we will:
- Delete your personal information from our active databases
- Retain anonymized data for analytical purposes
- Keep transaction records as required by law (7 years in Tanzania)
